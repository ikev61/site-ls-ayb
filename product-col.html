Product

Columbite - Niobium-Tantalum Ore

Overview

We provide premium-grade Columbite (Coltan), a vital ore containing niobium and tantalum. Sourced from the mineral-rich regions of Nigeria, our Columbite undergoes rigorous processing to meet the strict demands of the high-tech, aerospace, and medical device sectors worldwide.

Product Specifications

  • Chemical Composition: (Fe,Mn)Nb2O6 / (Fe,Mn)(Nb,Ta)2O6
  • Niobium Pentoxide (Nb2O5): 30% - 60%
  • Tantalum Pentoxide (Ta2O5): 5% - 25%
  • Form: Concentrated mineral sand or crushed ore
  • Color: Black, brown, or iron-black
  • Moisture Content: Less than 3%
  • Density: 5.3 - 7.3 g/cm³
  • Packaging: 50 kg sealed drums or bags

Industrial Applications

  • Electronics: High-capacity tantalum capacitors for smartphones, computers, and medical equipment
  • Aerospace: Superalloys for jet engines and turbine blades
  • Medical: Surgical implants and pacemakers (Tantalum is highly biocompatible)
  • Steel Industry: High-strength, low-alloy (HSLA) steel production

Quality Control & Assurance

All shipments undergo thorough testing for metal content and impurities. We provide third-party assay reports (SGS, Bureau Veritas, or equivalent) to ensure strict compliance with buyer requirements. Samples can be provided upon request prior to bulk orders.

Export & Logistics Information

  • Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ): 25 - 50 Metric Tons (varies by mineral)
  • Supply Ability: Up to 5,000 MT/month
  • Delivery Terms: FOB / CIF / Ex-Works
  • Shipping: Via sea from certified Nigerian export terminals (e.g., Apapa, Tin Can)
  • Documentation Provided: Certificate of Origin, Mineral Assay Report, Export License, Bill of Lading, and Commercial Invoice.
