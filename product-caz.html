Product

Cassiterite (Tin Ore)

Overview

Ayudidibe Mega Ventures (Nig) Limited is a leading supplier of high-grade Cassiterite, the primary ore of tin (Sn). We mine, process, and export quality tin-bearing ore sourced from secure and environmentally responsible deposits. Our Cassiterite is essential for global industries ranging from electronics to automotive manufacturing.

Product Specifications

  • Chemical Formula: SnO2
  • Tin (Sn) Content: 50% - 70% (Customizable based on client needs)
  • Form: Raw ore or crushed concentrate
  • Color: Brown, black, or reddish-brown
  • Moisture Content: Less than 5%
  • Density: 6.8 - 7.1 g/cm³
  • Packaging: 50 kg bags or 1MT jumbo bags

Industrial Applications

  • Electronics: Manufacturing solder for circuit boards and electronic components
  • Tinplate Production: Corrosion-resistant coating for steel packaging (e.g., food cans)
  • Chemicals: Production of tin compounds for plastics, ceramics, and glass manufacturing
  • Alloys: Bronze, pewter, and bearing alloys

Quality Control & Assurance

All shipments undergo thorough testing for metal content and impurities. We provide third-party assay reports (SGS, Bureau Veritas, or equivalent) to ensure strict compliance with buyer requirements. Samples can be provided upon request prior to bulk orders.

Export & Logistics Information

  • Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ): 25 - 50 Metric Tons (varies by mineral)
  • Supply Ability: Up to 5,000 MT/month
  • Delivery Terms: FOB / CIF / Ex-Works
  • Shipping: Via sea from certified Nigerian export terminals (e.g., Apapa, Tin Can)
  • Documentation Provided: Certificate of Origin, Mineral Assay Report, Export License, Bill of Lading, and Commercial Invoice.
